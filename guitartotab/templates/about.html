About

GuitarToTab converts WAV audio files to guitar tablature. It is ideal for short snippets of audio ranging from 5-10 seconds. This app is meant to be used as a "notebook" for short guitar riffs.

To use the app make an account and then select "New Tab" from the navbar. Then choose between recording audio or uploading pre-existing audio. After choosing a title and uploading audio, press "Create Tab"

For editing/ deleting tabs simply click on the tab title while on the "My Tabs" page.
